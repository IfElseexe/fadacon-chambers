About the Chambers

Dedicated to protecting your rights

Fadacon and CG Chambers is a full-service firm of counsel and advocates committed to delivering exceptional legal services across diverse practice areas: from the courtroom to the boardroom, and from land transactions to alternative dispute resolution.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of law does Fadacon and CG Chambers specialise in?
The Chambers is engaged in general criminal and civil practice, military law, admiralty law, corporate and real property matters, conveyancing and alternative dispute resolution (ADR). See our practice areas for detailed write-ups on each.
How do I book a consultation?
Use the "Tell us about your matter" form on this page and our counsel will contact you promptly. You may also call or WhatsApp 0706 341 5883, or visit the Chambers at PEB 04 Plaza, Wuse Zone 5, Abuja.
Do you handle land agreements and change of ownership?
Yes. Property transactions are a core part of our practice, land agreements, conveyancing, documentation, change of ownership and perfection of title.
Do you handle military legal matters?
Yes. Military law is one of our distinctive specialties, and our principal has extensive experience advising and representing clients in military legal matters.
What should I bring to my first consultation?
Bring any documents relevant to your matter, agreements, letters, court processes, receipts or identification. If you are unsure, come with what you have; we will guide you on the rest.
Is my information kept confidential?
Absolutely. All enquiries and consultations are treated in strict professional confidence, in line with our obligations as legal practitioners. See our Privacy Policy for details.

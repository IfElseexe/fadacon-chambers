About the Chambers

Dedicated to protecting your rights

Fadacon and CG Chambers is a full-service firm of counsel and advocates committed to delivering exceptional legal services across diverse practice areas — from the courtroom to the boardroom, and from land transactions to alternative dispute resolution.

The Chambers was established as the manifestation of a dream: to serve humanity by contributing to society through the practice of law. That founding conviction guides how we treat every client and every matter, no matter its size.

Our Principal

Imegu Christopher, Esq.

LL.B · LL.M · B.L  |  Called to the Nigerian Bar, 2003

With over two decades of active legal practice, Imegu Christopher leads the Chambers with deep experience spanning criminal and civil litigation, military law, corporate and real property matters, conveyancing and alternative dispute resolution.

His practice is founded on a simple conviction: the law exists to serve people. Every matter the Chambers accepts — from a land agreement to complex litigation — receives the same rigour, candour and personal attention.
